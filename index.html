Do you already know the virtual reality glasses?

Anyone who has read or heard about this metaverse knows that this is one of the main bets of giant technology companies (big techs) for the future of online interaction and even the internet. To access these fully virtual worlds, however, you need not only a computer or cell phone, but also virtual reality (VR) glasses. That's because, unlike the common internet, the metaverse is based on the concept of avatars and digital environments, in which you access a simulation of a universe in three dimensions.
